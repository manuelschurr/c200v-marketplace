# Writing Samples & Portfolio Pointers

What you can show someone evaluating you, and how to frame each piece.

## Portfolio Site
> URL, source repo (if any), what's on it, how confident you are in the current version.

## Blog Posts / Long-Form Writing
> One subsection per post. For each:
> - What it's about
> - What it demonstrates (skill, perspective, taste)
> - When to highlight (which role types or company contexts it fits)
> - Status (published, draft, private)

## GitHub / Code Samples
> Which repos are public and worth showing? How well-documented are they? Anything that should be polished before linking from a job application?

## Media Appearances
> Podcasts, interviews, talks, conference appearances.

## Other Writing
> Strategy docs, product specs, internal memos you can share (anonymized if needed).

## What's Missing
> Writing samples or artifacts you wish you had but don't. Anything you should create before the job search gets serious.

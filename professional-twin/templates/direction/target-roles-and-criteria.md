# Target Roles & Criteria

What you're looking for in your next role, and what would make you walk away.

## Role Types
> What roles are you targeting? (Title and seniority. List in priority order.)
> For each: what does the ideal version look like? What scope, what team size, what level of ownership?
> What's the minimum role you'd accept? Where's the line between "interesting step down" and "no"?

## Hard Criteria (Dealbreakers)
> Things that would make you walk away regardless of how good the rest looks. Be specific — these get checked first when evaluating any opportunity.
- Remote / hybrid / office requirements?
- Compensation floor?
- Location constraints?
- Company stage exclusions?
- Industry exclusions?
- Notice period or start date constraints?

## Strong Preferences (Not Dealbreakers, But Close)
> Things that aren't absolute but matter a lot.
- Agency / autonomy — how much, and what does "enough" look like concretely?
- Team quality signals?
- Tech culture / engineering quality?
- Growth trajectory of the company?
- Product maturity (zero-to-one, scaling, optimization)?
- Reporting line preferences?

## Nice-to-Haves
> Things that would make an opportunity more attractive but aren't requirements.

## Anti-Patterns
> What have you seen in past roles that you now know to avoid? Red flags from job descriptions or interview processes. Organizational structures or cultures that don't work for you.

## Open Questions
> Anything unresolved.

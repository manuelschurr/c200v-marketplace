# Common Application Questions & Answers

Reusable answers to questions that come up repeatedly in applications and interviews. Each answer should have a "base" version and notes on how to adapt it.

## Motivation & Direction
- Why are you looking for a new role?
- Where do you see yourself in 3-5 years?
- What are you looking for in your next company?
- Why [this company]? (Template / approach, not a single answer.)

## Leadership & Management
> If applicable to your role type.
- Tell me about a time you built something from scratch.
- How do you handle conflict?
- Describe a situation where you had to influence without authority.
- How do you prioritize when everything is urgent?
- Tell me about a hard decision you made with incomplete data.

## Craft / Discipline-Specific
> Replace with the questions your discipline gets asked.
- Walk me through how you'd approach [problem in your field].
- Tell me about something you shipped that you're proud of. Why?
- Tell me about something that failed. What happened and what did you learn?

## Personal & Meta
- What are your strengths?
- What are your weaknesses? (Honest version + interview version.)
- Why should we hire you over other candidates?
- Tell me about yourself. (The 2-minute version.)

## Compensation
- What are your salary expectations?
- What's your approach to equity negotiation?

## Potentially Tricky Questions
> Anything specific to your history that you need a prepared answer for. Hard transitions, gaps, controversial departures.

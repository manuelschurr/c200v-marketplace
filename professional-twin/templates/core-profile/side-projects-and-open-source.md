# Side Projects & Open Source

Side projects demonstrate initiative, technical ability, taste, and what you care about outside of work. This doc captures what to highlight and how to frame each project depending on the audience.

## Projects

> One subsection per project. For each, capture:
> - **One-liner:** What it does in one sentence
> - **Status:** Active / Beta / Private / Archived, with dates
> - **Problem:** What real-world need or pain it addresses
> - **Solution:** What you actually built
> - **Tech:** Stack
> - **Your role:** What you contributed (especially for collaborative or co-founded projects)
> - **What it demonstrates:** The skill or trait an employer should take from it
> - **When to highlight:** Which roles or companies this is most relevant for
> - **Public link** (if any)
